Project Description
Created for an inbound marketing services company that helps businesses drive leads to their sales team through content nurturing, the logo mark is symbolic of an ongoing interaction between two parties and forms an "N" shape in the negative space. The website and collateral materials all have a clean and modern tone — designed to help project an identity of a bleeding-edge technology company.
